Kedijaandco was found in the year 2018 by a group of five young Tanzanian girls
They realised that Tanzanian artists are not being valued neither is the culture.
Kids nowdays do not even know the kind of art that Tanzania has in its culture.
This is mainly caused by the impact of Globalization to culture which dates
back to the era before and after colonialism where most Tanzanians had
already started adopting the the Western culture and Globalization
just catalysed it.One of the founders speaks of how she grew up
not knowing all of the art genre in Tanzanian culture. She
walked around Posta one day and met a random man who had
sketched her in five minutes in exchange of money.
That incident sparked a fire in her to help
talented Tanzanians have a plattform for
identifying artists, showcasing their
talents and selling their arts at
a reasonable price.
