MikonoArt is a platform to help artists in Tanzania showcase their talent.
The idea came after an experience someone had when she went out.
She walked around Posta one day and met a random man who had
sketched her in five minutes in exchange of money.
That incident sparked a fire in her to help
talented Tanzanians have a plattform for
identifying artists, showcasing their
talents and selling their arts at
a reasonable price.
